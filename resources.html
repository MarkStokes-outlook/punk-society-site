Start with safety

We are not medical professionals. If you or someone you know needs urgent help, contact local emergency services (999 UK), NHS 111, Samaritans (116 123), or your country’s crisis line immediately. These directories are for ongoing support, not emergency care.

Every recommendation below links straight to the organisation or creator. Always read their guidance directly so advice stays accurate, trauma-informed, and up to date.

How to use this page

  • Direct people to official diagnosis pathways and helplines.
  • Pair lived-experience content with charity/clinical references.
  • Update links whenever new partners, clinics, or creators emerge.

Source files live in research/nd-organisations.md and research/nd-creators.md. Keep them current before editing this page.
